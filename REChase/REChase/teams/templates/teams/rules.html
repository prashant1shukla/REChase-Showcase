RULES

  1. There will be several levels with increasing difficulty.
  2. A player can only be in one team.
  3. One player will create the team and another member can join by entering the team code.
  4. Once you join/create a team you cannot leave.
  5. Answers can be multiword. You need to gives spaces between them.
  6. Answers aren't case-sensitive.
  7. You cannot proceed to the next level without completing the current level.
  8. Hints will be released as per the number of teams stuck on a particular question.
  9. Refresh the page to see hints.
  10. Seeing a hint won't cost any penalty.
  11. Leaderboard will be decided according to the time taken by teams to complete levels.
